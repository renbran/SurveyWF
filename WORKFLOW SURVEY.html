CAD team corrects drawings, recalculates areas, fixes formatting. If the issue requires re-measurement, field surveyor may need to revisit the site. Senior surveyor re-verifies all corrections. Typical turnaround: 1‚Äì3 days.

Paid to DLD/ADM/RAK for the survey application. Fee depends on type: AED 500 (land), AED 600 (villa/unit), AED 3,000 (building), AED 5,000 (common areas/component plan). Plus AED 20 knowledge + innovation fees.

üöó Field Operations Costs

‚àíVariable (fuel, per diem, equipment wear)

Phase 7 ‚Äî Office Processing

üíª Labor Cost ‚Äî CAD/GIS Processing

‚àíLabor hours √ó hourly rate

The most significant internal cost. CAD draftsmen spend 2‚Äì7 days processing data into drawings and reports. This is where time tracking becomes critical for profitability analysis.

Phase 9 ‚Äî Submission to Authority

üíµ Second Milestone Payment

+40% of Contract Value

When the survey report is submitted to the authority, client pays the second milestone (40%). This is a key billing trigger ‚Äî proof of submission is provided to the client.

Phase 9 ‚Äî Rectification (if needed)

üîß Rectification Costs (Hidden Cost!)

‚àíUnplanned labor hours

‚ö†Ô∏è This is the biggest profitability killer. Each rectification round requires CAD re-work, QA re-review, and admin re-submission. These hours are rarely billed to the client but cost the company real money. Tracking this in Odoo Timesheet is critical.

Phase 10 ‚Äî Final Delivery

üíµ Final Payment & Project Close

+30% of Contract Value (Balance)

Upon certificate issuance and delivery of all documents to the client, the remaining 30% balance is invoiced and collected. Project is financially closed.
