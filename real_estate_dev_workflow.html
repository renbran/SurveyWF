Handover & Snagging
‚è± 2‚Äì6 months
Complete construction, obtain completion certificate, conduct unit inspections (snagging), rectify defects, hand over keys to buyers.

üìã Key Activities

  • Apply for Building Completion Certificate (BCC) from Municipality
  • Obtain final NOCs: Civil Defense, DEWA/ADDC, Etisalat/Du, drainage authority
  • Internal pre-handover inspection: developer QA team walks every unit

‚öôÔ∏è Odoo Actions

  • Helpdesk: Each snag item = ticket, categorized (MEP/Finishing/Structural), assigned to contractor
  • Helpdesk SLA: 7-day fix SLA for minor snags, 14-day for major ‚Äî auto-escalation
  • Project: "Handover ‚Äì [Unit ID]" task with checklist: NOCs ‚úì, inspection ‚úì, snag clear ‚úì, keys ‚úì
  • Sales Order: Mark SO as "Delivered" upon key handover
  • Invoicing: Final installment invoice generated and sent to buyer
  • Accounting: Release retention to contractor after DLP (scheduled action)

Accounting & Finance

FINANCE ¬∑ ALL PHASES
Escrow accounting: Separate journal for escrow bank account. Buyer payments credit escrow liability; drawdowns debit on RERA approval.
Revenue recognition: IFRS 15 ‚Äî recognize revenue at point of transfer (handover) or over time (% completion method).
Retention accounting: 5-10% of contractor invoices held as retention liability. Released after 12-month DLP (scheduled journal entry).
Multi-currency: Handle AED + USD transactions for imported materials. Automatic exchange rate updates.
Budgets: Project budgets in analytic accounting. Real-time budget vs. actual reports by cost center.

Helpdesk

AFTER-SALES ¬∑ P7 ¬∑ P8
Snagging tickets: Each defect = ticket with photo attachment, category (Plumbing/Electrical/Finishing), severity, assigned contractor.
SLA policies: Minor defect: 7 days. Major: 14 days. Critical (water leak): 24 hours. Auto-escalation to PM on breach.
Maintenance portal: Owners/tenants submit maintenance requests online. Auto-assign to facility management team.
Knowledge base: FAQ articles for common queries: parking rules, gym timings, move-in procedures, NOC for renovations.

Maintenance

FACILITIES ¬∑ P8

Preventive maintenance: Recurring calendar: elevator servicing (monthly), fire alarm testing (quarterly), generator load test (bi-annual).
Corrective maintenance: Breakdown request ‚Üí diagnose ‚Üí spare part PO ‚Üí fix ‚Üí close. Cost tracked per equipment.
MTBF/MTTR tracking: Mean time between failures and mean time to repair ‚Äî identify equipment needing replacement.
